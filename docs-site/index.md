## Why G-Rump?

G-Rump is more than just another AI coding assistant. It's a complete development workflow that helps you:

- **Think through problems** with structured SHIP methodology
- **Generate architectures** before writing code
- **Produce production-ready code** with best practices baked in
- **Maintain consistency** across your entire codebase
